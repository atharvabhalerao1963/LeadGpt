# 🚀 LeadGPT — AI Business Intelligence Platform

LeadGPT is an AI-powered Business Intelligence and Sales Analytics platform that helps businesses analyze leads, generate AI insights, forecast trends, and interact with sales data using natural language.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ Lead Quality Prediction
- ✅ AI Business Insights
- ✅ RAG Chatbot
- ✅ Conversational Analytics
- ✅ AI Recommendation Engine
- ✅ Forecasting Dashboard
- ✅ PDF Business Reports
- ✅ Interactive Plotly Dashboards
- ✅ FastAPI Backend
- ✅ Streamlit Frontend

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

### Frontend
- Streamlit
- Plotly

### Backend
- FastAPI
- Pandas
- Scikit-learn

### AI / ML
- LangChain
- ChromaDB
- HuggingFace Embeddings
- Groq LLM
